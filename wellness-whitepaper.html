The Solution: The Gut-Mind Reset Program (GMRP)

The report proposes the GMRP, a 12-18 month framework to systematically restore the gut-mind axis. Explore the three distinct phases of the program using the tabs below to understand how the solution is structured for sustainable, long-term results.

Phase 1: Microbiome and Metabolic Reset

Duration: Months 1–3

This initial phase focuses on eliminating inflammatory triggers and intensely nourishing the gut. Through a whole-foods, high-fiber diet and targeted supplementation (probiotics, prebiotics, vitamins, minerals), the primary goal is to correct dysbiosis and replete nutrient deficiencies, creating a stable foundation for future progress. Intermittent fasting is intentionally avoided to prevent additional stress on the body.

Phase 2: Habit Formation with Phased IF

Duration: Months 4–9

Once the gut is stabilized, this phase introduces intermittent fasting (IF) as a tool to enhance metabolic flexibility and cellular repair. IF is introduced gradually (e.g., starting with 12:12 once per week) and is supported by digital Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) to manage hunger cues and solidify new habits.

Phase 3: Long-Term Maintenance

Duration: Months 10–18 and Beyond

The final phase transitions the user to a sustainable, flexible lifestyle. The program adapts to a maintenance protocol with less reliance on supplementation and more emphasis on intuitive eating within the established healthy framework, ensuring long-term adherence and resilience.
