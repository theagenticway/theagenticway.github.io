The Italian South: Gaeta

The ultimate authentic beach start. Clear water, historic medieval streets, and world-class pizza without the massive tourist crowds.

Basecamp 1

Beachfront Serapo Apt

A bright, spacious apartment in "New Gaeta." With an elevator, fully equipped kitchen, and two balconies, it's perfect for a family of 6. Supermarkets and bakeries are right downstairs.

🏖️ Beach Access

5-minute flat walk to Lido Sirio/Lido Nave.

🛒 Convenience

5-Day Itinerary

Day 1: The Arrival

Wed, June 4

Land at NAP (18:00). Quick taxi to Napoli Centrale, train to Formia (21:20), and taxi to your Gaeta Airbnb to settle in.

🍽️ Dining Highlight

Pizzeria del Porto

Loud, authentic, family-friendly. Perfect for your first traditional vegetarian Italian pizza at 9:30 PM.

Day 2: Serapo Beach

Thu, June 5

Rent umbrellas at Serapo Beach. Relax, swim in the clear water, and enjoy the evening passeggiata (stroll) on Corso Italia.

🍽️ Dining Highlight

Tiella di Gaeta (Beach Kiosks)

Grab Gaeta's famous stuffed pizza pie for lunch. The Scarola (endive) and Olive version is naturally vegetarian and delicious.

Day 3: Boat to Sperlonga

Fri, June 6

4-hour private/group boat tour (09:30-13:30). Swim in the Blue Grotto and view the whitewashed town of Sperlonga from the sea.

🍽️ Dining Highlight

Magie dei Sapori

Excellent local pasta spot. Request the Pasta alla Norma (eggplant) or classic Cacio e Pepe. Cook family dinner at the Airbnb later.

Day 4: History & Boutiques

Sat, June 7

Morning hike at Montagna Spaccata. Afternoon boutique shopping in Piazza Traniello (Acqua di Gaeta, Lady Yachting).

🍽️ Dining Highlight

Ristorante La Rete & Il Pinguino

Traditional lunch (ask for Spaghetti Aglio e Olio), followed by evening gelato at Gaeta's famous Il Pinguino.

Day 5: Pure Relax

Sun, June 8

A completely open day to recover before the big travel day. Rent a Pedalò with a slide for the boys, pack, and empty the fridge.

🍽️ Dining Highlight

Airbnb Family Dinner

Use up your groceries for a relaxing balcony dinner as the baby sleeps early.

The Grand Lakes: Stresa

Old-world elegance on Lake Maggiore. Palaces, white peacocks, island-hopping, and quick access to Milan's fashion district.

Walk out the door to catch boats to the islands.

4-Day Itinerary

Day 6: The Roman Transfer

Mon, June 9

Train to Rome (10:12). Store bags, take a 3-hr taxi tour (Colosseum/Vatican). Catch 15:50 Frecciarossa to Milan, then to Stresa (Arr 20:36).

🍽️ Dining Highlight

Rifugio Romano (Rome) & Mamma Mia (Stresa)

Lunch in Rome at a spot known for Vegan Carbonara. Grab easy, reliable veg pizza at Mamma Mia upon arriving exhausted in Stresa.

Day 7: The Island Princes

Tue, June 10

Private Hop-On Hop-Off boat tour (10:00-15:00). Explore Isola Bella's manicured gardens and white peacocks, then head to Isola Madre.

🍽️ Dining Highlight

Ristorante Verbano (Isola Pescatori)

The best terrace on the lake for lunch. They accommodate vegetarians beautifully with fresh risottos. Book well in advance!

Day 8: Mottarone Coasters

Wed, June 11

Guided cable car trip up the mountain. 5 runs each on the Alpyland Alpine Coaster. Afternoon swim at Lido Blu.

🍽️ Dining Highlight

Lo Stornello

Located in the pedestrian alley back in Stresa, featuring a dedicated vegetarian menu focusing on local Piedmontese ingredients.

Day 9: Milan Fashion Run

Thu, June 12

Fast train to Milano Centrale (08:00). Explore the independent boutiques of the Brera district (Emily Levine, Vibi Venezia). Back to Stresa by 15:30.

🍽️ Dining Highlight

Flower Burger (Brera, Milan)

Incredibly fun, 100% vegan colorful burgers that the kids will go crazy for, right in the shopping district.

The Swiss Hub: Thun

The gateway to the high Alps. A brilliant logistical base with a surfing river, castles, and direct train access to the giants of Grindelwald.

🚞 Rail Access

Short walk to Thun station for daily Alpine trips.

4-Day Itinerary

Day 10: The Simplon Crossing

Fri, June 13

Train from Stresa to Domodossola. Pre-booked Taxi bridge to Iselle (to bypass construction). Board the 12:18 train to Thun. Explore Thun's Mühleplatz.

🍽️ Dining Highlight

Ristorante La Pergola

A fantastic family-friendly Italian spot in Thun where you can reliably get high-quality vegetarian pasta to fuel up for hiking.

Day 11: Grindelwald Giants

Sat, June 14

Train to Grindelwald. Take the First Gondola, walk the steel Cliff Walk, hike to Bachalpsee, then cross to Männlichen for the Giant Cow Playground.

🍽️ Dining Highlight

Coop Picnic & Beau-Rivage

Lunch is a picnic of Swiss cheeses and bread on the mountain. Dinner back in Thun at Beau-Rivage for great pizza.

Day 12: The Blue Lake

Sun, June 15

Train to Kandersteg. Ride the gondola up to Oeschinensee. Rip down the mountain on the Rodelbahn (coaster) and hike to the bright blue alpine lake.

🍽️ Dining Highlight

Alpine BBQ & Chillounge

Bring veggie sausages to grill at the lake's public fire pits for lunch. Dinner at Chillounge in Thun for international bowls and river views.

Day 13: 72 Waterfalls

Mon, June 16

Train to Lauterbrunnen. Walk the valley to Staubbach Falls, then go inside the mountain to see the roaring Trümmelbach Falls. Sunset trip to Harder Kulm.

🍽️ Dining Highlight

Airtime Café (Lauterbrunnen)

The absolute best spot for vegetarians in the valley. Hearty soups, great sandwiches, and excellent coffee.
