Vegetarian Dining Guide

Curated options emphasizing fresh vegetables, egg-free, and plant-based specialties.

🌅 Breakfast

  • Bar Bazzanti

    Corso Cavour 100. Classic espresso and Italian pastries (cornetti).

  • Pasticceria Stenta

    Via Indipendenza. Historic bakery perfect for grabbing quick bites before a hike.

☀️ Lunch

  • Tiella Kiosks (Serapo)

    Grab the "Scarola e Olive" (Endive and Olive) Tiella pie. Naturally vegan and a local staple.

  • Magie dei Sapori ↗

    Via Indipendenza. Request traditional Cacio e Pepe or Pasta alla Norma (eggplant/tomato).

🌙 Dinner

  • Pizzeria del Porto

    Via Indipendenza 64. Fast, loud, authentic Neapolitan pizza. Fantastic Margherita and Marinara.

  • Radio Bottega ↗

    Piazza Traniello. Hip spot with incredible cheese/vegetable boards and robust salads.

🌅 Breakfast

  • Caffè Torino

    Piazza Cadorna 39. Old-world cafe on the main square. Great fresh juices and coffee.

  • Gigi Bar

    Corso Italia 30. Lakefront views, famous for their "Margheritine di Stresa" cookies.

☀️ Lunch

  • Ristorante Verbano ↗

    Isola Pescatori. Best terrace on the lake. They specialize in local Risotto (egg-free/fish-free available).

  • Mamma Mia

    Via Principe Tomaso 11. Very easy, reliable spot for vegetarian pizzas and salads with kids.

🌙 Dinner

  • Lo Stornello ↗

    Via Cavour 35. High-end traditional. They offer a dedicated vegetarian menu featuring Piedmontese veggies.

  • Osteria Degli Amici

    Via Anna Maria Bolongaro 33. Cozy setting with great truffle pastas and minestrone.

🌅 Breakfast

  • Mani's Coffee & Bagels ↗

    Panoramastrasse 1. Fantastic vegan/veg bagels, excellent coffee, very close to your Airbnb.

  • Äss-Bar ↗

    Bälliz 64. Sustainable bakery concept selling yesterday's pastries at a massive discount.

☀️ Lunch

  • Hitzberger (Station) ↗

    Right at Thun train station. Fast, healthy vegetarian wraps and quinoa bowls for hike days.

  • Airtime Café (Lauterbrunnen) ↗

    Fuhren 442. For your waterfall day: the absolute best vegetarian sandwiches and soups in the Alps.

🌙 Dinner

  • Ristorante La Pergola

    Obere Hauptgasse 25. Excellent Italian in Thun with reliable, fresh vegetarian pasta options.

  • Chillounge

    Mühleplatz. Great riverside vibe offering Mexican/international veggie bowls and nachos.

🌅 Breakfast

  • Ou Bien Encore ↗

    Rue des Bains 61. A fully vegetarian/vegan haven. Incredible tofu-based dishes, acai, and plant-based treats.

  • Boréal Coffee Shop ↗

    Rue du Stand 60. Artisanal coffee and fresh, simple pastries.

☀️ Lunch

  • Alive ↗

    Rue des Barques 2. 100% Vegan superfood cafe. Beautiful, healthy, and high-energy food.

  • Manora (Manor Dept. Store)

    Rue de Cornavin 6. Top floor buffet with incredible fresh vegetable stations, customized stir-fry, and panoramic views.

🌙 Dinner

  • Les Armures ↗

    Rue du Puits-Saint-Pierre 1. The most famous spot in the Old Town for traditional Swiss Cheese Fondue (naturally veg).

  • En Faim ↗

    Rue des Eaux-Vives. Small, cozy spot specializing in creative vegetarian and vegan gastronomy.

🏛️ Rome (Layover)

  • Rifugio Romano (Lunch) ↗

    Via del Castro Pretorio 54 (Near Termini Station). A MUST VISIT. They specialize in fully Vegan versions of classic Roman pasta (Vegan Carbonara, Cacio e Pepe). You must book in advance.

  • Mercato Centrale (Termini)

    Inside the station. High-end food hall with tons of artisan vegetarian pizza, pasta, and veggie options if you are short on time.

👗 Milan (Day Trip)

  • Flower Burger (Lunch) ↗

    Viale Vittorio Veneto 10. Incredibly fun, 100% vegan colorful burgers (pink/purple buns) that the 4 boys will absolutely love while shopping.

  • Obicà Mozzarella Bar (Brera) ↗

    Via Mercato 28. High-end vegetarian pizzas and tasting boards of Italy's best mozzarella.

Expanded Activities Guide

Extra options, side quests, and en-route highlights with pricing for the family.

🏖️ Gaeta & Coast

  • Villa of Tiberius (Sperlonga) ↗

    Explore the ancient Roman emperor's grotto and museum right on the beach.

    ~ €5.00
  • Sanctuary of Montagna Spaccata

    Hike the "Split Mountain" and visit the Turk's Cave.

    Free / Donation

🏛️ Rome (En-Route)

  • Colosseum Exterior / Forum ↗

    If you just take a taxi to the outside for photos, it is free. Inside requires advanced tickets.

    Inside: ~ €18.00
  • The Pantheon ↗

    The best preserved ancient Roman building. Quick to see during your 3-hour layover tour.

    ~ €5.00

👗 Milan (Day Trip)

  • Leonardo da Vinci Science Museum ↗

    Incredible models of da Vinci's machines. A perfect diversion for the 4 boys while shopping.

    ~ €10.00
  • Duomo Terraces ↗

    Take the elevator to the roof of the massive cathedral for incredible gothic architecture views.

    ~ €16.00 - €25.00

🛥️ Stresa & Lakes

  • Villa Pallavicino Park ↗

    A botanical garden combined with a free-roaming zoo (deer, llamas, birds). Great for kids.

    ~ €13.00
  • Rocca di Angera ↗

    Take a ferry across the lake to this imposing medieval castle housing a massive doll/toy museum.

    ~ €14.00

🏔️ Thun & Alps

  • St. Beatus Caves ↗

    Explore deep mountain caves featuring underground waterfalls. Good rainy day alternative.

    ~ CHF 19.00
  • Thun Castle ↗

    The iconic white castle overlooking your Airbnb. Features a historical museum inside.

    ~ CHF 10.00 (Kids free w/ family)

✈️ Geneva

  • CERN Science Gateway ↗

    Brand new, highly interactive visitor center for the Large Hadron Collider particle physics lab.

    FREE
  • Patek Philippe Museum ↗

    Five centuries of watchmaking history and mechanical automata.

    ~ CHF 10.00
