Family Planning: Multi-Generational Itineraries

Big Island Oahu Maui

The Big Island

Best for adventure, space, and unifying "Farm-to-Table" dining. Accessible sightseeing for Grandparents, huge nature for the boys.

Where to Stay

Four Seasons Hualalai

The gold standard. Villa-style rooms (great for seniors), and the "King's Pond" (swimmable lava aquarium) for the kids.

Mauna Lani (Auberge)

Modern luxury. Calm beach cove protected from waves. Home to CanoeHouse, the island's best restaurant.

7-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Lava Pools

Settle in. Boys snorkel in the resort lagoon; Grandparents relax on the lanai.

Oahu

Best for variety, history (Pearl Harbor), and concurrent high-end menus for mixed diets.

Where to Stay

Four Seasons Ko Olina

Protected lagoons (safe swimming), removed from city noise. Near Disney Aulani for character breakfast option.

The Kahala Resort

Iconic luxury near Waikiki but secluded. Has its own private dolphin lagoon.
